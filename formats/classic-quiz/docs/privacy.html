Privacy Policy - Guess the Pokémon

Last updated: 2026-07-11

"Guess the Pokémon" is an automated content project that generates and publishes short quiz videos (type chart, cry, ability, silhouette, reveal) built from public PokeAPI data. This page describes how the project's publishing automation handles data.

Who operates this

This project is operated by an individual developer (contact below). It is not a company and does not offer any product or service to end users - it exclusively posts pre-generated video content to its own social media accounts (YouTube, TikTok).

What data is processed

The publishing automation only uses OAuth credentials (access/refresh tokens) belonging to the operator's own YouTube and TikTok accounts, in order to upload videos and manage a playlist. No data belonging to end users, viewers, or third parties is collected, stored, or processed. The automation does not use cookies, does not run any web-facing service, and does not collect analytics on viewers.

Data from PokeAPI

Video content is generated from public, non-personal Pokémon data provided by PokeAPI (pokeapi.co). No personal data is involved in this process.

Third-party platforms

Videos are published to YouTube and TikTok using each platform's official API under the operator's own developer/account credentials. Handling of any data by YouTube/TikTok themselves is governed by their own privacy policies.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to romanspeer@web.de.
