Terms of Service - Guess the Pokémon

Last updated: 2026-07-11

"Guess the Pokémon" is an automated content project that generates and publishes short "guess the Pokémon" quiz videos to the operator's own YouTube and TikTok accounts, using an app registered with each platform's developer program.

Purpose and scope

This app is used exclusively by its operator to publish self-generated video content to accounts the operator owns. It does not act on behalf of any other user, does not accept content from third parties, and does not expose any public-facing service, API, or login flow to end users.

Content

All video content is generated programmatically from public data provided by PokeAPI (Pokémon names, types, type matchups, abilities, official artwork/sprites, and cry audio) plus background music. Pokémon and related trademarks are property of Nintendo, Game Freak, and The Pokémon Company; this project is a non-commercial fan project and is not affiliated with or endorsed by them.

Background music

Background music consists of lo-fi Pokémon-inspired arrangements created and self-produced by CinderyLofi. The creator's own video description grants free use of these tracks in videos and other content, provided credit is given. In line with that, every video's description credits and links to the CinderyLofi channel.
